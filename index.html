Features

seeSpeech is a desktop GUI app designed convert recordings or text into ISL. Our solution is aimed towards increasing accessibility in schools committed to having an inclusive approact to education.

Speech to ISL

Preparing for an assembly? Don't want hearing-impaired students to feel left out? Use our solution to prepare an ISL translation of a recording.

Text to ISL

Preparing for a webinar? Don't want hearing-impaired students to feel left out? Use our solution to prepare an ISL translation of any script well in advance.

Real time translator (beta)

Live class? We got you covered! Use our solution to transcribe and translate speech into ISL in real time! (Beta feature)

At the forefront of inclusivity

At smartnUp, we're committed to enhancing the educational experience. This goes hand-in-hand with making education as inclusive as possible. seeSpeech is a product aimed at doing just that.

Fast

Expansive Dictionary

Our dictionary is 3000 words long! In any case, if a word is not identified, it'll be finger-spelled using standard ISL alphabet signs, ensuring no loss in translation!
